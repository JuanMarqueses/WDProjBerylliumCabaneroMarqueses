Overview

Founded in 1946, the Golden State Warriors have won multiple NBA championships and have played a major role in shaping modern basketball. The franchise rose to prominence in the 2010s, redefining the game through ball movement, spacing, and elite shooting. With a strong commitment to player development and winning culture, the Warriors remain a respected and competitive organization in the league
