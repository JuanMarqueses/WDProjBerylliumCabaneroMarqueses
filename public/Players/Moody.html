Moses Moody

    Full Name Moody
    Position Shooting Guard
    Height 6'5"
    Weight 211 lbs
    Team Golden State Warriors

    Moses Moody is known for his 3point shooting ability, scoring versatility, and length on the wing for the Golden State Warriors. He is an essential scorer for the warriors as his main role is to make the most shots whether close or long range, he usually gets open from far in plays in order to take free shots and score for his team. He isa reliable contributor for the Warriors, shooting efficiently from deep, spacing the floor, and helping defensively against perimeter players. During recent seasons hes averaged around 10 to 11 points per game while providing solid rebounding and playmaking for his position. Moody also helped the Warriors win the NBA championship early in his career and is contining to develop into a key role player for the team alongside stars like Stephen Curry
