Dray

    Full Name Draymond Green
    Position Power Forward
    Height 6'6"
    Weight 230 lbs
    Team Golden State Warriors

    Draymond Green is a highly versatile and high-energy NBA player known for his leadership and defensive prowess. He excels at guarding multiple positions, making him one of the leagues top defenders. Beyond defense, he is a skilled playmaker who can pass, set screens, and initiate the offense when needed. Green is also recognized for his intensity, and ability to motivate teammates on and off the court. Offensively, he contributes with timely scoring and smart shot selection. Overall, Green is the engine of his team, combining toughness, intelligence, and unselfish play to impact every facet of the game. As a power forward he is typically the most aggressive in the team with the goal to break defense. He is known to cause fights often gaining technical fouls and is sometimes ejected from the game.
