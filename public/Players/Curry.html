Chef Curry

s
Full Name Stephen Curry
Position Point Guard
Height 6'2"
Weight 185 lbs
Team Golden State Warriors

Stephen Curry is widely regarded by many as one of the greatest shooters in basketball's history. His role as the point guard is being to make sure his team gets every possible scoring oppurtinity and that they may execute players requiring him to have extravagent leadership to guide the other players .As the cornerstone of the Golden State Warriors, he changed the game with his deep three-point range and quick release. Known for his ball-handling, court vision, and clutch performances he was given the nickname "Chef Curry". Curry has won multiple NBA championships and MVP awards. Beyond his scoring ability, he is respected for his leadership, great work ethic, and impact on modern basketball.
