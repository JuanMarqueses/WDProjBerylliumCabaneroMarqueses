Q

    Full Name Quinten Post
    Position Center
    Height 7'0"
    Weight 238 lbs
    Team Golden State Warriors

    Quinten Post is a skilled and versatile NBA center known for his size, strength, and basketball IQ. He anchors the defense, protecting the rim, contesting shots, and securing rebounds on both ends of the floor. Offensively, he can score in the post, finish around the rim, and sometimes stretch the floor with mid-range or three-point shooting. Post is also valued for his ability to set screens and facilitate ball movement, helping his teammates get open looks. Despite not being as know as the rest of the team his presence in the paint makes him a key factor in both team defense and offense. His main role is useful as he charges to the paint for shots that are hard to block giving less effort on scoring.
