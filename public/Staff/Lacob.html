Joseph Steven Lacob

s
Born January 10, 1956
Position Co-Executive Chairman & CEO
Ownership of GSW Since 2010
Team Golden State Warriors
Championships as Owner 2015, 2017, 2018, 2022

Joe Lacob is the majority owner of the Golden State Warriors and has been a driving force behind the transformation of the franchise int one of the most successful modern NBA teams. Since buying the team in 2010, he has emphasized innovation, long-term planning, and investment in both player development and facilities. Under his leadership, the organization built a championship culture and moved into the Chase Center, helping raise the team's global profile and business success.
