Stephen Douglas Kerr

s
Born September 27, 1965
Position Head Coach
Coaching in GSW Since 2014
Team Golden State Warriors
Championships as Coach 2015, 2017, 2018, 2022

Steve Kerr is the head coach of the Golden State Warriors and one of the most successful coaches of his generation. A former NBA player and champion, he is known for applying a fast-paced, team-oriented playing style built on ball movement and three-point shooting. Since becoming head coach in 2014, Kerr has lead the team to multiple championships, and is heavily respected for his leadership, communication skills, and ability to let talent grow.
