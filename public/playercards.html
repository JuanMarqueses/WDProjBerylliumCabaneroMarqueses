2025 Starter Players

The Golden State Warriors’ roster is built on a foundation of skill, versatility, and strong basketball intelligence. Players on the team are known for their ability to shoot efficiently, create scoring opportunities for teammates, and contribute defensively, resulting in a fast-moving and unselfish style of play.

By combining veteran leadership with emerging young talent, the Warriors maintain a competitive edge in the league. This balance allows the team to remain successful in the present while also developing players who can contribute to the franchise’s future.
